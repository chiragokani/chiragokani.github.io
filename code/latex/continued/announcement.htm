Grad Skills seminar: LaTeX continued

Friday, Oct. 24 at 3 PM in ETC 4.150


Hello all, 

It's time for another Grad skills seminar this semester. This time, Chirag Gokani will talk about using LaTeX for different purposes in your research. The seminar will take place this Friday, October 24, at 3:00 p.m. in ETC 4.150.

The seminar will be a review and continuation of Chirag's “Introduction to LaTeX” Grad Skills seminar from last year. We encourage first-year students to review last year’s content, summarized on this webpage, before attending Friday’s seminar. Here is the outline for the session: 
  1. Brief review of introductory topics from last year’s seminar
  2. Typesetting using the JASA and UT dissertation templates.
  3. Making slides in Beamer
  4. Creating HTML pages with equations using MathJax
  5. Using KlatexFormula and LatexIt for equations in email and PowerPoint
  6. Questions and discussion
Thanks and looking forward to see everyone!
