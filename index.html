Hello! I am a PhD candidate in the Graduate Program in Acoustics at the University of Texas at Austin, where I am co-advised by Profs. M. R. Haberman and M. F. Hamilton. I study the scattering and diffraction of waves in problems with broken symmetry.

I enjoy engaging with the acoustics community as a student member of the Acoustical Society of America (ASA). I currently lead the ASA Student Council.

I share my wonder for waves through WiSTEM and the Austin Student Chapter of the ASA.
