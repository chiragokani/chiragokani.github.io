"The heavenly bodies are nothing but a continuous song for several voices (perceived by the intellect, not by the ear); a music which...sets landmarks in the immeasurable flow of time. It is therefore, no longer surprising that man, in imitation of his creator, has at last discovered the art of figured song, which was unknown to the ancients. Man wanted to reproduce the continuity of cosmic time...to obtain a sample test of the delight of the Divine Creator in His works, and to partake of his joy by making music in the imitation of God."

—Johannes Kepler, Harmonices Mundi

Wicked Game

This is a cover of Chris Isaak's 1989 classic that features my friends.

The Beautiful Music of Yamuna Vora

In 1991, my mother recorded my grandmother, Yamuna Vora, singing several hours of devotional songs (bhajans). In 2015, I converted the surviving hour-long cassette to MP3 and presented this with my 10th grade family history paper. In 2021, thirty years after the original recording, my extended family helped me translate the bhajans from Gujarati to English. I hope this musical and spiritual treasure can be cherished for years to come.

Guitar: My College Journey

This is the page where I present my Collegium V Honors Capstone project, a requirement to graduate with honors at UT Dallas. The project was completed in the spring 2021 semester under the direction of Dr. Enric Madriguera, Russell Cleveland Professor in Guitar Studies. This serves as an easy, self-contained way for me to share my project with my family and friends.

Ode to Friends

This is an ongoing creative project that celebrates my incredible friends. It combines physics, music, and philosophy. My friend Solvay Linde enhanced the project by adding a visual art component. The sounds and sights on this page continue to help me process the lives of those dear to me.

Orphan Girl

This is a cover of a Gillian Welch song that features my friends.

Transcription of "Forgotten Love"

This composition by Jaco Pastorius has baffled me since high school. I finally hashed it out in Prof. Kelly Durbin's MUSI 2319 Digital Music I class. Performed in Logic Pro.

Straight Eighths

Straight Eighths is the second studio album of original contemporary jazz by the C3 Big Band, led by Chris Clark and Michael Medrick. I play electric bass on tracks 4, 6, 7, and 8. The album features some of DFW's finest jazz musicians.

Guitar in Spain: 2019

I traveled to Spain in June-July 2019 to study classical guitar. It was a life-changing experience.

Albums with Stephen Houpt

I am honored to be featured on four studio albums by Stephen Houpt. Stephen's music is transcendent and inspiring. He is a world-class lap-style guitarist and is one of the most original and free musicians I have ever heard.
