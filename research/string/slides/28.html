Conclusion

  • Mathematically modeled the touch of Montgomery and Pass

  • Solved the 1D lossy wave equation with bending stiffness

  • Effect of initial displacement and velocity conditions on the timbre was characterized using \(\beta_\mathrm{rms}\)

  • Results suggest that Montomery and Pass achieve a focused but rich timbre by balancing \(D\) and \(\Delta\) with the chosen right-hand technique
