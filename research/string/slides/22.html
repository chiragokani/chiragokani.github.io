Simulations and discussion

  • Simulations are performed for G string (\(f_1 = \omega_1/2\pi = 196\) Hz)

  • \(\ell = \) \(0.648\) m \(=\) scale length of Gibson L-5

  • \(T = 177.1\) N and \(r= 3.3\times 10^{-4}\) m.*

  • \(E \simeq 198\) GPa.†

  • These values yield \(B \simeq 2.5\times 10^{-4}\)

  • \(\Gamma_n = 5\times 10^{-4}\) qualitatively recovers decay of guitar string

*D'Addario 13-56 medium flatwound chrome strings
†Polak et al. 2018
